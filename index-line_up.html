  • Abbath

    Abbath is a Norwegian black metal band formed in 2015 by Olve Eikemo, better known by his stage name Abbath Doom Occulta, following his departure from the influential black metal band Immortal. The band is named after its founder and is known for its intense and dark musical style, characterized by heavy guitar riffs and powerful vocals. Based in Bergen, Norway, Abbath has released several albums and continues to be a prominent name in the black metal scene.
